Daily Article

The Upward Call in the New Covenant

In the New Covenant era, God’s call is not limited to prophets in the Old Testament sense. Through the Holy Spirit, every believer hears the upward call in Christ Jesus (Philippians 3:14). Yet the initial call—the sound from above—is to salvation: “Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest” (Matthew 11:28, NIV).
