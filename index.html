About ATONE Chapel International

ATONE Chapel International is a Christ-centered prophetic ministry committed to transforming lives through the Word of God, prayer, worship, and the power of the Holy Spirit. We believe in salvation, restoration, deliverance, and the manifestation of God's glory in every believer.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To raise a holy and empowered people who walk in dominion, purpose, and intimacy with God.

Mission: To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ with power, equip believers for kingdom service, and impact nations through prophetic ministry and love.
