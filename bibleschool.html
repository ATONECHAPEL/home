Admission Requirements

Atone Bible School is open to believers who are hungry for growth, discipline, and spiritual responsibility. Admission is based on calling, character, and commitment.

Spiritual Readiness

Applicants must be born again, committed to prayer, and willing to grow in holiness and obedience.

Commitment to Training

Students must attend classes faithfully, complete assignments, and submit to mentorship.

Character & Discipline

A teachable spirit, respect for leadership, and godly character are required.

Evangelistic Heart

A willingness to preach the gospel, serve others, and represent Christ publicly.
