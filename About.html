Welcome to Atone Chapel

Atone Chapel is a prophetic ministry founded by Prophet Joseph Adarkwah under the divine mandate to raise a generation that walks in dominion power through atonement, prayer, and the prophetic word of God.

We are a covenant community where lives are transformed through the blood of Jesus Christ, deep worship, accurate prophetic ministry, and the teaching of sound doctrine.

Our Vision

To establish a covenant altar where believers experience true atonement, redemption, and empowerment to exercise dominion in every sphere of life.

Our Mission

• To preach the gospel of atonement and reconciliation through Christ
• To raise prophetic voices and intercessors for the end-time harvest
• To equip believers with revelatory knowledge of God's Word
• To demonstrate the power of God through signs, wonders, and miracles

Our Leader

Prophet Joseph Adarkwah is the visionary and senior prophet of Atone Chapel. Called by God with a strong prophetic anointing, he ministers with accuracy, power, and compassion, bringing deliverance, healing, and breakthrough to thousands.

His ministry is marked by deep revelation, apostolic authority, and a burning passion for soul-winning and kingdom advancement.
